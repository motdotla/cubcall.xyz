Get the app

Add CubCall to your phone

Right now CubCall works in Safari — add it to your home screen in 3 easy steps!

1
Open cubcall.xyz in Safari
Make sure you're using Safari on your iPhone — it won't work in Chrome or other browsers.
2
Tap the Share button
That's the little box with an arrow pointing up at the bottom of your screen.
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the menu and tap it — CubCall will appear on your home screen just like a real app!
